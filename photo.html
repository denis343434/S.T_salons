Service Description

We are providing a complete package through multiple activities on the 4-hour Quad Desert Safari: Sandboarding, Camels & Pictures. Drive across the sand dunes by ATV (Quad), you can enjoy a camel ride, sandboarding as well as many other exciting activities.

Starting with the pick up from your hotel in a 4x4 Vehicle you will head off with your local guide to the desert. After a 45min-60min drive from Dubai, you will reach the Lahbab Red Dunes desert, you will get 20–25 minutes to relax and to do your camel ride there.

Restroom and toilet facilities are also available.

Once you are ready, the ride into the red dunes with your own Quadbike (Kawasaki- 350CC) will start. Instruction and safety-gear will be provided. Continuing with 1 hour Quadbiking is an unforgettable experience. Your guide will help you to find the best track in desert. You can also try sandboarding, a popular desert activity and take as many pictures as you want in the beautiful unlimited desert.

After your Activity we will provide you with cold water & softdrinks and bring you back to your Hotel safely.
